Problem Understanding

Core Problem:

Academic resources on campus are siloed â€” great notes, past papers, and solved assignments get lost after exams. Students spend hours hunting for materials that already exist elsewhere. There's no trusted, searchable, community-driven repository.

Key User Insight:

There are two personas â€” the Contributor (has materials, wants recognition) and the Seeker (needs materials, short on time). A successful platform serves both simultaneously and rewards the contributor loop so the flywheel keeps spinning.

Why Current Solutions Fail:

WhatsApp groups have no search. Google Drive links expire. Library systems are bureaucratic. Existing platforms (StudyLib, Scribd) aren't campus-specific and have no community trust layer.
